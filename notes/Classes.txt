Every class upgrades their pistol every 10 floors, and gains 1 DEF upon room clear
**Soldier** (5 HP, 3 DEF)
+ Recover 2 DEF, rather than 1, upon clearing a room
+ Recover 1 HP when entering a floor
**Scout** (3 HP, 2 DEF)
+ Increased movement
+ Dodges have a shorter cooldown
**Tinkerer** (3 HP, 4 DEF)
+ Gain extra ammo when entering a floor
+ Upgrade your pistol every 5th floor
